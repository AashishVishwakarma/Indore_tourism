“ The biggest and most popular mall in the city, Treasure Island is your one-stop-destination for shopping at the best international brand stores, trying out a range of cuisines and spending fun times with the kids. The recently renovated mall hosts luxury brands like Tommy Hilfiger, Nautica, GAP etc and also has a thrilling gaming zone for the kids ”

“Sarrafa Bazar, or ornamentalists' market is an old market near Rajwade, in heart of Indore city. In day time, it's a market for jewellery, but post shutting down of these shops in the night, a street food Market took over in front of these shops. The likes of indian street food from fried, boiled, baked, ripe, raw foods could be found here. Must try are coconut water shake, pani puri, tikki, poha and milk. Don't go here before 10 pm like me, else you'll find very little options. The market gets its glory post 10 pm. It's a crowded place so expect a good walk to reach here.”

— Sarafa-Bazaar-Indore,
